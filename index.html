ToT — What to Include

Your ToT is your personal guide and decision filter throughout the program.

  • A clear description of the shift you want to make in how you lead.
  • Specific behaviors you want to see more or less of.
  • The biggest barrier you anticipate.
  • The impact you aim to create as a result of this transformation.
  • What strengths will support your transformation.
  • What organizational resources, departments or individuals will support your growth.

Format is your choice: video, audio, or written.
